Main Roles in Films

The Smashing Machine

Dwayne Johnson (as Mark Kerr)

One of the world's highest-grossing actors, Dwayne "The Rock" Johnson transitions from global action star to take on the complex, dramatic role of MMA legend Mark Kerr. His past works include the 'Fast & Furious' and 'Jumanji' franchises.

Emily Blunt (as Dawn Staples)

An acclaimed actress known for her versatility, Emily Blunt has starred in a wide range of films like 'A Quiet Place', 'Oppenheimer', and 'The Devil Wears Prada'.

Benny Safdie (Director)

Known for his intense, character-driven films, Benny Safdie has directed critically acclaimed projects like 'Uncut Gems' and 'Good Time', often collaborating with his brother Josh.

Jolly LLB 3

Akshay Kumar & Arshad Warsi

This film brings together two stars of the franchise. Akshay Kumar, known for a vast filmography of action and comedy hits, reprises his role alongside Arshad Warsi, who is celebrated for his impeccable comic timing, most famously as 'Circuit' in the Munna Bhai series.

Subhash Kapoor (Director)

A former journalist, Subhash Kapoor is the writer and director behind the satirical 'Jolly LLB' franchise, known for blending social commentary with humor.
